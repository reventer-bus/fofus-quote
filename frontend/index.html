PROCESS

Three steps. One quote.

No signup. No waiting. No email back-and-forth. Drag in your file, choose your machine, see the price.

01
Upload your 3D file
STL, OBJ or 3MF. We read the geometry right in your browser — nothing leaves until you confirm. Max 100 MB.
02
Pick a printer and material
Bambu A1, X1 Carbon, Creality K1 Max. PLA, PETG, ABS, TPU, engineering blends. Set infill and quality. We check the build volume fits before you commit.
03
See your price. Send to printer.
Weight, print time, material cost, machine cost, total — broken out line by line. Hit "Request Printing" and our backend slices it with OrcaSlicer, queues it on the fleet.
