🧪
Silicone Casting
We 3D print a mould from your design, then cast silicone into it. Ideal for gaskets, custom seals, soft-touch grips, and skin-safe applications. Food-grade and skin-safe options available only with certified materials — please ask. Price: custom quote based on part size and silicone type.
🚗
Fibre-Reinforced Parts
We print your part in PETG for dimensional accuracy, then reinforce it with industrial resin and fibre layers for strength. Ideal for impact-resistant brackets, covers, and functional parts. Price: custom quote based on part size and reinforcement layers. Not recommended for structural or safety-critical automotive components without independent testing.
