PROCESS

Three steps. One quote.

No signup. No waiting. No email back-and-forth. Drag in your file, choose your machine, see the price.

01
Upload your 3D file
STL, OBJ or 3MF. We read the geometry right in your browser — nothing leaves until you confirm. Max 100 MB.
02
Pick a build chamber and material
Three chamber sizes: Standard 256³, Engineering 256³, Large 300³. PLA, PETG, ABS, TPU, engineering blends. Set infill and resolution. We check the build volume fits before you commit.
03
See your price. Send to printer.
Weight, print time, material cost, machine cost, total — broken out line by line. Hit "Request Printing" and our backend slices it with the FOFUS production engine, queues it on the fleet.
