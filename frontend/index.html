PROCESS

Three steps. One quote.

No signup. No waiting. No email back-and-forth. Drag in your file, choose your material, see the price.

01
Upload your 3D file
STL, 3MF or OBJ. We read the geometry right in your browser — nothing leaves until you confirm. Max 100 MB.
02
Pick your material and quality
Choose from PLA, PETG, ABS, Silicon (mold-cast), or Fibre-coated (PETG + resin + fibre for automobile parts). Set infill and resolution from 0.08 mm (ultra-fine) to 0.40 mm (draft). We check the build volume fits before you commit.

See your price. Send to printer.
Weight, print time, material cost, machine cost, total — broken out line by line. Hit "Request Printing" and our backend slices it with the FOFUS production engine, queues it on the fleet.

Silicon Parts
We 3D print a precise mold of your part, then cast food-grade or skin-safe silicon into it. Ideal for gaskets, custom seals, soft-touch grips, and medical/skin-safe applications.
🚗
Fibre-Reinforced Parts
We print your part in PETG for dimensional accuracy, then coat it with industrial resin and fibre layers for strength. Ideal for automobile brackets, covers, and structural components that need impact resistance.
