# FOFUS B2B WhatsApp Outreach Templates

**Policy:** These are for individual, personalised outreach only. No bulk broadcast lists. Each message must be customised with the recipient's business name and a plausible reason for contact. Maximum 1 message per business per week unless they reply.

---

## 1. Architects / Interior Designers

**Template:**
> Hi [Name], I'm from FOFUS 3D Prints in Thrissur. We produce architectural scale models, interior maquettes, light diffusers, and custom fixtures on demand. Saw [Business Name] on Google — thought our service might be useful for your client presentations. Can I send a few photos of our work?

**When to send:** Morning 10–11am, Tuesday–Thursday.

---

## 2. Gift Shops / Trophy Shops

## Follow-up (only if no reply after 5 days)

**Template:**
> Hi [Name], just following up on my earlier message. No pressure — happy to send a photo catalogue if there's interest. If not, I'll close the loop here.

---

## Rules

1. Always use the recipient's real business name.
2. Never claim a prior relationship that does not exist.
3. Do not send the same message to multiple businesses unchanged.
4. If they say "not interested" or "stop", mark them DND and never message again.
5. Do not send payment links or raw UPI IDs in the first message.
6. First message should ask permission before sending catalogue/photos.
7. Keep it under 2 WhatsApp message bubbles.
