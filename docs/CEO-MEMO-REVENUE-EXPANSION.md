## Recommended Executive Decision

### 1. Immediately scale **B + D + E** (services, scanning, ads)
These are the fastest revenue levers that do not dilute the FOFUS brand.

### 2. **Pause C (platform rental)** until the following questions are answered:
- Will the rented platform use the FOFUS brand name or a separate brand?
- Who owns customer support, print quality disputes, and refunds?
- What SLA do we guarantee for uptime and print success rate?
- What stops a rental partner from competing directly with us?
- How is our IP (pricing model, slicer calibration) protected?

**If we rent the platform, it must be under a different brand name and legal entity.** Same technology, different customer-facing name. Otherwise FOFUS brand quality becomes hostage to a third party’s service level.

### 3. Keep **A** running, but shift marketing spend toward higher-AOV services (B2B custom prints, idols, moulds).

- Store is live. Quote site is live. Checkout works.

### Proposed budget
- **Test phase:** ₹500/day for 7 days = ₹3,500
- **Scale phase:** If ROAS > 2.5×, increase to ₹1,000/day
- **Total July 21–Aug 18 test budget:** ₹15,000–₹30,000

1. **Performance Max → store.fofus.in** (product catalog)

3. **Search → B2B terms** ("architectural scale model Thrissur", "custom moulds Kerala", "3D printed gifts Kerala")

### Expected outcome
- At ₹1,000/day with 2.5× ROAS → ₹2,500/day revenue
- This alone closes ≈60% of the daily gap.

## Platform Rental — Decision Framework

If the executive team wants to proceed with renting the platform, the CEO recommends:

1. **Create a separate brand** (e.g., "PrintMesh", "LayerOne", "FabGrid") — not FOFUS.
2. **Separate Shopify / domain / logo** from store.fofus.in.
3. **Separate support email and WhatsApp number.**
4. **Start with one trusted operator** as a paid pilot (₹10,000–₹15,000/month) with a 3-month contract.
5. **Audit clause:** FOFUS can terminate if their customer ratings fall below 4.0.
6. **No access to FOFUS customer data or Shopify store.**
